Disclaimer. This self-assessment is indicative and for informational purposes only. It does not constitute legal advice or a formal compliance determination, and has no binding effect in any procurement procedure. The objective definitions, SEAL levels and the "lowest-level-wins" rule follow the Commission's published Implementation Guidance. The category weights (SOV-1 20%, SOV-2 10%, SOV-3 10%, SOV-4 15%, SOV-5 10%, SOV-6 15%, SOV-7 15%, SOV-8 5%) are the exact values from the Commission's official Sovereignty Assessment Calculator and remain adjustable. Because that calculator scores 43 individual questions while this tool rates each objective at a single SEAL level, the per-objective score is derived from the calculator's actual per-answer values (mean value per SEAL band), not reproduced question by question. The illustrative answer scores in the spreadsheet are noted by the Commission as fictitious.
Source: European Commission — Cloud Sovereignty Framework, Implementation guidance (June 2026). Built as an independent assessment aid; not affiliated with or endorsed by the European Commission.
